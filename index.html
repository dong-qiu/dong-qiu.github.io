I am current a PhD candidate in the ISEU, School of Computer Science and Engineering at Southeast University. My advisor is Prof. Bixin Li. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Southeast University in 2008. My research interests span software analysis and testing, regression testing and monitoring in web services & SOA, database applications and programming languages.
